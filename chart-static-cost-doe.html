Fuel Cycle Costs

These figures show the cost of different fuel cycle options, broken down into the following categories:
  1. Capital Costs: The costs to construct all the nuclear reactors that are operating in the fuel cycle.
  2. Operating Costs: The costs to operate all the nuclear reactors that are operating in the fuel cycle.
  3. Fuel Cycle Costs: All the costs involved in the management of nuclear fuel except dispotal.
  4. Disposal Costs: The costs to dispose of all the waste material from the nuclear fuel cycle.
